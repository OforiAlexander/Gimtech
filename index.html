History of Kalamed College
of Health Sciences

n 2020, a visionary dream took root in Ghana. Driven by the desire to address the growing need for qualified professionals in healthcare, management, and technology, philanthropist Haruna Musah Kalamulai founded Kalamed College of Health Sciences. Inspired by his success with the Kalamed Pharmacy chain, Mr. Kalamulai envisioned an institution that would transcend traditional boundaries, empowering individuals to excel in diverse fields and contribute meaningfully to their communities.
